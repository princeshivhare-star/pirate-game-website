Rule the Seven Seas

Pirate Raids

Board enemy vessels in brutal ship-to-ship combat. Fire broadside cannons, outmaneuver your foes, and take their cargo as the spoils of war.

🏛️
02 — World
Living Ports

Walk through bustling harbor towns. Enter taverns, markets and warehouses. NPCs trade, argue and move naturally in every port you visit.

📜
03 — Trade
Contracts & Cargo

Take delivery jobs between ports for guaranteed coin. Or gamble on the free market — buy cheap, sell high, exploit price differences across routes.

Sail a full world map spanning continents. Unlock new ports as your legend grows, revealing distant waters with richer rewards and deadlier pirates.

A living economy responds to your actions. Flood a market and prices crash. Corner a trade route and watch your rivals scramble for scraps.

Experience epic sea battles from sweeping cinematic angles — wide ocean vistas, dramatic ship interiors, and tight harbour close-ups.
